## Playoff Format & Rules

- All rounds best-of-7
- Bracket re-seeded after each round (highest remaining seed vs lowest)
- Home court: Higher seed hosts games 1, 2, 5, 7 (2-2-1-1-1 format)
- Road team hosts games 3, 4, 6

### Tiebreaker Rules (regular season seeding)
1. Head-to-head record
2. Division record (if same division)
3. Conference record
4. Record vs. playoff-bound conference teams
5. Record vs. playoff-bound opposite conference teams
6. Point differential (capped ±10/game)
