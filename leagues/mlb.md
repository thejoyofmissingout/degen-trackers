## MLB Playoff Format & Rules

### Structure (6 teams per league, 12 total)
| Round | Format | Home field |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| Wild Card Series | Best-of-3 | Higher seed hosts ALL 3 games |
| Division Series (ALDS/NLDS) | Best-of-5 | Higher seed: 2-2-1 |
| League Championship Series | Best-of-7 | Higher seed: 2-3-2 |
| World Series | Best-of-7 | AL/NL alternates by year |

### Who Makes It
- 3 division winners per league (seeds 1-3 by record, not just winning division)
- 3 wild card teams per league (seeds 4-6 by record among non-division-winners)
- **No. 1 seed gets bye through Wild Card round** — only 5 teams play Wild Card

### Wild Card Series Rules
- Best-of-3, all 3 games at higher seed's park
- Starting rotation matters hugely — teams set ace for Game 1
- No off-days — 3 games in 3 days

### Tiebreaker Rules
- **No tiebreaker game** (eliminated as of 2022)
- Head-to-head record is first tiebreaker
- Then H2H within division
- Then win % in last 162 games
- Then coin flip (extremely rare)

### Home Field Advantage (World Series)
- Alternates by league — check each year which league hosts Game 1

## Weather & Outdoor Game Notes

### Wind Archetypes (MLB-specific)
- **Wind IN 10+ mph + ERA 4.00+ starters** → Lean UNDER (#17)
- **Wind OUT 12+ mph + both starters ERA 4.50+** → Lean OVER (#18)
- **Temp below 45°F** → Lean UNDER (#19)
- Early April games at northern parks (Chicago, Minneapolis, Detroit, Cleveland) — cold suppresses scoring

### Parks Most Weather-Affected
- Wrigley Field (CHC) — wind off Lake Michigan, biggest park-factor swings in MLB
- Fenway Park (BOS) — wind off Charles River, Green Monster factor
- Guaranteed Rate Field (CWS) — Chicago wind
- Target Field (MIN) — April cold, exposed to elements
- Progressive Field (CLE) — April cold

## Scheduling Archetypes — MLB Specifics

### Day Game After Night Game (DANG — Archetype #9)
- Especially dangerous in April when teams travel long distances
- Home team playing 7pm followed by 1pm next day = stale lineup

### West-to-East Travel (Archetype #13)
- Teams flying from LA/SF/SEA to East Coast for early games
- 3-hour time difference, early start = real fatigue disadvantage

### Extra Rest vs Short Rest (Archetype #5)
- In early season with doubleheaders and off-days, rest edges appear often
- Track carefully in April when schedule bunching is common
