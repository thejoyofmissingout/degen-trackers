## Playoff Format

| Round | Format | Higher Seed HCA | Lower Seed HCA |
|-------|--------|-----------------|----------------|
| First Round | Best-of-3 | G1, G3 | **G2** |
| Semifinals | Best-of-5 | G1, G2, G5 | G3, G4 |
| Finals | Best-of-7 | G1, G2, G5, G7 (2-2-1-1-1) | G3, G4, G6 |

- Top 8 teams qualify by overall W-L record (no conference seeding — seed 1 vs 8, 2 vs 7, 3 vs 6, 4 vs 5)
- No play-in tournament
- **No reseeding** between rounds — bracket advances as set
- Finals expanded to best-of-7 in 2025 (was best-of-5 prior)

### Tiebreaker Order (seeding)
1. Head-to-head record among tied teams
2. Win% vs. teams finishing .500 or better
3. Point differential in head-to-head games
4. Overall point differential
5. Coin toss / draw of lots

### Key Betting Angle — First Round G2
Lower seed hosts G2 of the first round. If the higher seed won G1, the lower seed is playing **elimination at home** in G2 — crowd intensity + desperation. This is unique to WNBA (NBA higher seed always has HCA). See Archetype #68.

## Betting Angles & Trends

### Historical ATS Patterns
| Angle | Rate | Edge |
|-------|------|------|
| Home underdogs ATS | 53.2% | Back home dogs, esp. mid-tier vs. elite favs |
| Road favorites ATS | 46.8% | **Best consistent WNBA fade** — road favs underperform |
| Unders | 53.1% historically | Lean unders, especially early season |
| #1 overall seed → champion | 8 of last 10 seasons | Chase whoever runs away with best record |
| Best regular season record = champion | 7 of last 10 | Strongest futures predictor |
| Western Conference titles | 8 of last 10 | East has only 2019 Mystics + 2012 Fever |

### 2026-Specific Angles

**1. Clark vs. Bueckers (4 games: May 9, Aug 14, Aug 20 + TBD)**
Public money floods both sides. Expect Fever to be overpriced on ML. Dallas at home dog value may appear. Most line movement of any non-Finals matchup.

**2. Expansion team early fade**
Portland Fire and Toronto Tempo will be novelty-bet by casual fans. Historically +EV to fade early. Exception: Brondello has Tempo competitive faster than typical expansion.

**3. Connecticut Sun — Lame Duck**
Final Connecticut season before Houston relocation. Motivation, fan support, and chemistry all uncertain. 500-1 is correct for futures. Individual game lines may not properly price the motivational chaos.

**4. Napheesa Collier Prop / Lynx Lines**
Biggest single market mover in 2026. When confirmed healthy: Lynx lines jump — strong OVER trigger for win total. When she misses games: fade MIN hard on the road. Do not bet Lynx win total until her status clarifies.

**5. NY Liberty — Sharps vs. Public**
Liberty are the public's team (+230 favorite, defending champs). Sharps will look to fade early while new coach DeMarco adjusts. Watch for early-season ATS underperformance = fade opportunity.

**6. FIBA Break Totals**
Condensed August schedule before break → fatigue → scoring dips. Post-break (Sep 16+): rested players, playoff push energy = lean OVERS in final regular season stretch.

**7. Commissioner's Cup (June 1-17)**
Games count toward regular season record. Teams push harder than public expects — treat as high-motivation. Teams eliminated from Cup may have lighter mid-June schedule = rest advantage in regular-season games.

**8. Back-to-Back Road Games**
WNBA schedules compressed vs NBA. Smaller rosters absorb fatigue worse. B2B road spots = reliable fade. Flag all road B2B games — most reliable scheduling edge in WNBA.

**9. Dallas Wings Rotation Instability**
Bueckers + Ogunbowale + Fudd = 3 elite guards, 2 spots. Role definition takes time. Fade Dallas in close games until rotation stabilizes. Fernandez is unproven as an in-game adjustor.

**10. Cameron Brink Return Watch (LA Sparks)**
When Brink is confirmed healthy and in rotation, Sparks' odds and lines shift materially. Monitor preseason workload. Her full health = Sparks are a legitimate contender.

### WNBA vs NBA Betting Differences

| Factor | WNBA | NBA |
|--------|------|-----|
| Season length | 44 games | 82 games |
| Typical total | 155–175 | 220–240 |
| Sharp action | Thin market | Heavy |
| Line movement | Slower, smaller | Fast, large |
| Injury reporting | Less media coverage | Immediate |
| Back-to-back impact | More severe (12-player roster) | Better absorbed |
| Line inefficiency | 3-5 pt spreads differ across books | Tighter |

**Line shop aggressively on WNBA** — DK/FD/BetMGM often disagree by 3-5 points. Thin market = more inefficiency to exploit.
